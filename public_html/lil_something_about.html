About lil Sleepy

Early Life

Zach Harby was an all American sleeper. As a young as the age of 1, Zach was competing in national sleeping competitions and would finish top 3 every event. During his middle school years Zach acquired a found love for rhymes. This new found love would take Zach down a path of fame.

Rap Career

With the rhyming skills Zach developed during his national rhyming competition years, he would take the rap culture by storm. The only thing that would stop Zach from full domination of the culture was his inability to show up early. This costed Zach many first place finishes due to his love for sleep. After high school Zach would take up the stage name as "lil Sleepy". Sleepy's first hit song was "Tick Tock it's Bed O'Clock" and it was number one on Chillboard's top 100 hits. Being only 18 and having almost as much fame as Ekard and Menime, fellow rap artists.

Sleep Career

Lil Sleepy is the worlds most notorious sleeper. Sleepy has lost many friends and family due to his addiction to sleep, but he still keeps showing the world why he is the best at what he does.
